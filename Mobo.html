Motherboard

Motherboard (also known as Mainboard, or Mobo) is the main circuit board inside the computer that connects the different components together and allows communication between the hardware. It has sockets for the CPU, Ram, expansion cards, and also connects the drives, cables and wires.


Different Sizes of Motherboards:

  • + ATX - Advanced Technology EXtended *
  • + Micro-ATX - Micro Advanced Technology EXtended *
  • + Mini-ITX - Mini Information Technology EXtended *
  • + Nano-ITX - Nano Information Technology EXtended
  • + Pico-ITX - Pico Information Technology EXtended

* Most used Motherboard Sizes on modern computer systems.
